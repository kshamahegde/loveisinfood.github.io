RECIPES

Aloo Nuggets

Wash the potatoes,boil them.Peel them off. Mash the boiled potatoes. Add red chilly powder, salt, chat masala,chopped garlic, corn flour and bind into a dough. Make small balls and deep fry them one by one in hot oil. Serve with ketchup.

Lays Masala

Heat a tava. spread the lays over it. Put chopped onions, chopped tomatoes, grated cheese,sprinkle red chilly flakes ,oregano and salt. Heat for 2 minutes.

Paneer Butter Masala

Heat a pan, add oil, clove, cinnamon, cardamom, green chillies, diced onions, diced tomatoes, ginger garlic paste. Add water and boil for 5 minutes. Cool down the mixturer and make a puree. Heat nother pan, add butter, salt,red chilly powder, diced paneer, fry the paneer and take it out. Then add more butter, onion-tomato puree, roasted paneer, 200ml water and heat it. Lastly add fresh cream, corriander leaves. And serve hot with roti,naan.
